This is why you should #vote4u:

The new Deutschlandticket massively improves the ticket-options available for affordable pubic transport. Furthermore, it is superior to the new JugendticketBW, as it can be used throughout Germany and is not limited to the state of Baden-Württemberg. Especially in our border region, where many students come from and live in Bavaria, a ticket that is valid in other states is necessary. I will put emphasis on point with the Uni Ulm management.

The student-teacher relationship is, by nature, one where power is situated on the teachers side primarely. This is why transparency about expectations, rules and exams is essential. The option to get legal advice is very limited at Uni Ulm so far. I will work on starting a new institution that can advice students on their rights and convey issues to teachers from a more neutral standpoint. A collaboration with universities in BW that have a legal class (e.g. Tübingen, Heidelberg) might be a possible starting point.

I will be in the senate as your representative. This is why I will continue to use this website to keep you informed about what's going on in the senate, as this university belongs to all of us. Students are just as important for the success of this institution as the teachers. You will also always be able to contact me here and bring issues forward.
