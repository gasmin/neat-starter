Career

Like many others, I started my career with various summer jobs as a highschool student. By working for a couple of weeks during my summer vacation, I was able to earn some money and spent it mostly on overprized technichal gadgets. Later, I spent half a year working at a local dentist, where I did simple tasks like cleaning and steralizing instruments.
After finishing school in 2021, I spent a year as a Corporate Student at Hewlett Packard Enterprise studying Business Information Systems in Stuttgart. Even though I am very greatful for everything I've learned during my time as a Dual Student, I realized that I just wasn't following my true passions.
This is why I took the risk and made the difficult decision to quit my job at HPE and applied for a spot as a medical student. Luckily, everything worked out in the end and now I am very greatful to be in my first semsester as a med student in Ulm.

To help fund my studies and give a little bit back to struggeling students like myself in middle school, I also work as a tutorer at the moment.
